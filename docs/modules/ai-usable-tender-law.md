# AI-Usable Tender Law Knowledge

The legal rules module prepares PetroProcure for AI-assisted tender law evaluation without giving AI final decision authority.

Current scope:

- Structured legal documents, articles, and clauses.
- Versioned procurement rules.
- Deterministic rule evaluator.
- Evaluation findings with legal references and rule version links.
- Search interfaces prepared for future retrieval.

Human responsibility:

- AI and rule evaluation are advisory only.
- Findings, warnings, recommendations, and legal references help users.
- Final approval remains with authorized users, managers, and commissions.

Future plan:

- PDF parsing to suggest article/clause extraction.
- Clause tagging by topic, severity, and applies-to scope.
- RAG/vector search over approved legal clauses.
- AI explanations grounded in retrieved legal clauses and active rule versions.
